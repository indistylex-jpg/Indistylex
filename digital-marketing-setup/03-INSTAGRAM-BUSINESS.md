# 03 — Instagram Business Account Setup

## What This Does
Converts your Instagram to a Business account with analytics, shopping, ads, contact buttons, and professional tools.

**Time needed**: 20 minutes

---

## Step 1: Create Instagram Account

### 1.1 Download & Sign Up
1. Download Instagram app (iOS/Android)
2. Sign up with **business email**: `hello@indistylex.com`
3. Choose username: `indistylex`
   - If taken, try: `indistylex.in`, `indistylex_kids`, `shopindistylex`

### 1.2 Switch to Business Account
1. Go to **Profile** → **Settings** ⚙️
2. **Account** → **Switch to Professional Account**
3. Select **"Business"** (not Creator)
4. Category: **"Children's Clothing Store"**
5. Connect to Facebook Page: **"Indistylex - Kids Fashion"**

## Step 3: Set Up Instagram Shopping

> ⚠️ **Requirements**: Business account + Facebook Catalog + Verified domain

### 3.1 Check Eligibility
- ✅ Sell physical products
- ✅ Comply with Instagram Commerce Policy
- ✅ Business is in a supported market (India ✅)
- ✅ Facebook Page connected
- ✅ Product catalog set up (from Meta Business guide)

### 3.2 Enable Shopping
1. Go to **Settings** → **Business** → **Shopping**
2. Select your Facebook Catalog: `Indistylex Products`
3. Submit for review (takes 1-3 days)

### 3.3 After Approval
- Tag products in posts (up to 5 per image)
- Tag products in Stories
- Create Shopping tab on your profile
- Products show price when tapped

### 6.1 Check Your Insights
1. Go to **Profile** → **Professional Dashboard**
2. Or tap **Insights** on any post

### 6.2 Key Metrics to Track Weekly:

| Metric | Target (Month 1) | Where to Find |
|--------|-------------------|---------------|
| Followers | 500+ | Profile |
| Reach | 5,000+/week | Insights → Overview |
| Engagement Rate | 3-5% | Insights → Content |
| Story Views | 100+/story | Insights → Stories |
| Website Clicks | 50+/week | Insights → Overview |
| Saves | 20+/week | Post Insights |

### 6.3 Best Performing Content
Check monthly which posts got:
- Most saves (people want to buy later)
- Most shares (viral potential)
- Most DMs (purchase intent)

## Step 7: Set Up Reels Strategy

### 7.1 Reel Types That Work for Kids Clothing:

| Type | Example | Expected Reach |
|------|---------|----------------|
| Outfit transitions | Kid changes outfits to music | High |
| Packing orders ASMR | Satisfying packaging videos | Medium-High |
| Before/After | Plain kid → styled kid | High |
| Size comparison | Same outfit on different ages | Medium |
| Customer reactions | Unboxing videos | High |
| Style tips | "3 ways to style this kurta" | Medium |

### 7.2 Reel Best Practices:
- Hook in first 1-2 seconds
- 7-15 seconds = best length for virality
- Use trending audio
- Add text overlays (many watch on mute)
- End with CTA: "Save for later" or "Link in bio"
- Post at peak times (8-9 PM IST)

### 7.3 Trending Audio Strategy:
1. Save trending audios when you hear them
2. Check Reels tab → trending section weekly
3. Adapt trends to kids clothing context
4. Original audio with voiceover also works well

## Step 10: Engagement Strategy

### Daily Engagement Routine (15 min/day):

1. **Reply to ALL comments** within 2 hours
2. **Reply to ALL DMs** within 1 hour  
3. **Like & comment** on 10 posts from target audience accounts
4. **Engage with** 5 posts using your hashtags
5. **Share** 2-3 relevant stories from others (tag them)

### Accounts to Engage With:
- Mom bloggers in India
- Parenting pages
- Kids photography pages
- Complementary brands (kids shoes, accessories)
- Local community pages
