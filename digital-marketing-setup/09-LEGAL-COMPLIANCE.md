## Step 1: GST Registration

### 1.1 When You MUST Register:
- Annual turnover exceeds ₹40 lakhs (₹20L for special category states)
- Selling on marketplaces (Amazon, Flipkart)
- Inter-state sales online
- **Even below threshold**: Recommended for credibility + input tax credit

### 1.2 How to Register:
1. Go to: https://www.gst.gov.in
2. Click **"Services"** → **"Registration"** → **"New Registration"**
3. Documents needed:
   - PAN Card (business owner)
   - Aadhaar Card
   - Business address proof (rent agreement/utility bill)
   - Bank account statement/cancelled cheque
   - Photos (owner)
   - Constitution of business (if partnership/company)

### 1.3 Registration Details:
| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Legal Name | Your registered name |
| Trade Name | Indistylex |
| State | Your state |
| Business Type | Proprietorship / LLP / Pvt Ltd |
| Business Activity | Retail Trade |
| HSN Code | 6111 (babies' garments), 6209 (children's garments) |

### 1.4 GST Rates for Kids Clothing:
| Item | HSN Code | GST Rate |
|------|----------|----------|
| Kids garments (selling price ≤₹1000) | 6111/6209 | **5%** |
| Kids garments (selling price >₹1000) | 6111/6209 | **12%** |

### 1.5 After Registration:
- File GSTR-1 (sales return): Monthly by 11th
- File GSTR-3B (summary): Monthly by 20th
- Use accounting software: Zoho Books / Tally / ClearTax

## Step 2: Business Registration Options

### Option A: Sole Proprietorship (Simplest)
- No registration needed (just GST + PAN)
- Lowest compliance
- Unlimited personal liability
- **Best for**: Starting out, testing the market

### Option B: LLP (Recommended for growing business)
- Register at: https://www.mca.gov.in
- Cost: ₹5,000-10,000 (via CA)
- Limited liability protection
- Tax efficient (no dividend distribution tax)
- **Best for**: ₹10L+ revenue, want legal protection

### Option C: Private Limited Company
- Register via MCA
- Cost: ₹10,000-20,000 (via CA)
- Separate legal entity
- Easier to raise investment
- Higher compliance
- **Best for**: Planning to scale big, raise funding

---

## Step 3: MSME/Udyam Registration (FREE)

### Benefits:
- Priority in government tenders
- Lower interest rates on business loans
- Subsidy on patent/trademark registration
- Protection against delayed payments
- Easier bank account opening

### How to Register:
1. Go to: https://udyamregistration.gov.in
2. Enter Aadhaar number
3. Fill business details:
   - Enterprise name: Indistylex
   - Type: Manufacturing / Services
   - NIC Code: 14101 (manufacture of wearing apparel) or 47711 (retail sale of clothing)
   - Investment: Your investment amount
   - Turnover: Expected annual turnover
4. Submit — get **Udyam Registration Number** instantly

## Step 5: Trademark Registration

### Why Register:
- Legal protection for "Indistylex" brand
- Prevent others from using your name
- Required for Brand Registry on Amazon/Flipkart
- ™ symbol immediately, ® after registration

### How to Register:
1. Search existing trademarks: https://ipindiaservices.gov.in/tmrpublicsearch
2. File application at: https://ipindia.gov.in
3. Or use services: LegalRaasta, Vakilsearch, IndiaFilings

### Details:
| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Mark | INDISTYLEX |
| Class | Class 25 (Clothing) + Class 35 (Retail) |
| Type | Word Mark + Logo (file separately) |
| Applicant | Your legal name/business |

### Timeline & Cost:
- Application filing: ₹4,500 (individual) / ₹9,000 (company)
- Examination: 1-2 months
- Publication: 4 months (opposition period)
- Registration: 12-18 months total
- Valid for: 10 years (renewable)

## Step 6: Consumer Protection Compliance

### E-Commerce Rules 2020 (India):
Your website MUST display:
- [ ] Full business name and address
- [ ] Contact details (email + phone)
- [ ] Grievance officer name + contact
- [ ] Return/refund/exchange policies
- [ ] Product details: Country of origin, manufacturer
- [ ] Total price including all charges
- [ ] Delivery timeline
- [ ] Payment methods and security

### Product Page Requirements:
Each product MUST show:
- Name and description
- Price (inclusive of taxes)
- Country of origin
- Manufacturer/importer details
- Size/weight details
- Care instructions
- Expiry date (if applicable)

### Grievance Officer:
Add to your website (Contact or About page):
```

Phone: [Your Number]
```
