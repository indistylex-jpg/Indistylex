## What You Need Before Starting

See the checklist below — gather these items FIRST:

### ✅ Business Information Needed:
- [ ] Business name: **Indistylex**
- [ ] Business category: Kids Clothing / Children's Apparel
- [ ] Business address (physical or registered)
- [ ] GST Number (if registered)
- [ ] PAN Card (business owner)
- [ ] Business email (e.g., hello@indistylex.com or business@indistylex.com)
- [ ] Phone number (dedicated business phone)
- [ ] Business bank account details

### ✅ Creative Assets Needed:
- [ ] Logo (high-res PNG, transparent background, 1000x1000px)
- [ ] Logo (square version for profile pictures, 400x400px)
- [ ] Banner image (1200x628px for Facebook cover)
- [ ] 10+ product photos (white background, good lighting)
- [ ] 5+ lifestyle photos (kids wearing clothes)
- [ ] Brand colors defined (see `../digital-marketing/02-BRAND-GUIDE.md`)

### ✅ Accounts You'll Create:
- [ ] Google Account (dedicated business Gmail)
- [ ] Facebook personal account (admin for business page)
- [ ] Instagram account
- [ ] Razorpay/PayU merchant account
- [ ] Brevo (Sendinblue) account
- [ ] Canva account
- [ ] WhatsApp Business account
