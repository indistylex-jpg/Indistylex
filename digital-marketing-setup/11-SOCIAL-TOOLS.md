## Step 1: Canva Setup

### 1.1 Create Account
1. Go to: https://www.canva.com
2. Sign up with business email
3. Choose **"Small Business"** when asked

### 1.2 Set Up Brand Kit
1. Go to **Brand Hub** (left sidebar)
2. Add:
   - **Logo**: Upload your logo files
   - **Colors**: 
     - Primary: `#1A1A1A` (Black)
     - Accent: `#C9A94E` (Gold)
     - Background: `#FFFFFF` (White)
     - Light: `#F8F8F8` (Gray)
     - Urgent: `#E53E3E` (Red for sales)
   - **Fonts**:
     - Heading: Playfair Display
     - Body: Inter

### 1.3 Create Templates (Design Once, Reuse Forever)

Create these templates and save to "Brand Templates" folder:

| Template | Size | Use For |
|----------|------|---------|
| Instagram Post | 1080x1080 | Product posts, quotes |
| Instagram Story | 1080x1920 | Daily stories |
| Instagram Reel Cover | 1080x1920 | Reel thumbnails |
| Facebook Post | 1200x630 | FB feed posts |
| Facebook Cover | 820x312 | Page cover |
| WhatsApp Status | 1080x1920 | WhatsApp stories |
| Product Card | 1080x1080 | Product showcase |
| Sale Banner | 1080x1080 | Offer announcements |
| Carousel Post | 1080x1080 (multi) | Educational/tip posts |
| Email Header | 600x200 | Newsletter headers |

### 1.4 Template Designs for Indistylex:

**Product Post Template:**
- White/pastel background
- Product image (center, large)
- Brand name (top corner, small)
- Product name (bottom)
- Price in gold accent color
- "Shop Now" CTA

**Sale/Offer Template:**
- Black background (or brand dark)
- Gold text for "SALE" / discount percentage
- White text for details
- Urgency element ("24 Hours Only")
- CTA button style element

**Quote/Engagement Template:**
- Soft pastel or white background
- Playfair Display heading
- Relatable parenting quote
- Brand watermark (corner)

### 1.5 Content Batching Workflow
Every Sunday, create the week's content:
1. Open Canva → Your templates
2. Create 5-7 post designs (30 min)
3. Create 7 story designs (15 min)
4. Download all → Upload to Later/Buffer
5. Write captions in one sitting (20 min)

## Step 2: Content Scheduling Tool

### Option A: Later (Recommended for Beginners)

1. Go to: https://later.com
2. Sign up free
3. Connect accounts:
   - Instagram
   - Facebook
   - Pinterest
4. Free plan: 30 posts/month per platform

**How to Schedule:**
1. Upload media to **Media Library**
2. Drag to calendar slot
3. Write caption
4. Add hashtags (save hashtag groups!)
5. Set time → Auto-publish

### Option B: Buffer

1. Go to: https://buffer.com
2. Free plan: 3 channels, 10 posts/queue

### Option C: Meta Business Suite (Free, Unlimited)

1. Go to: https://business.facebook.com
2. **Content** → **Posts & Stories** → **Create Post**
3. Schedule for Facebook + Instagram together
4. Unlimited scheduling (but no Pinterest/other platforms)

## Step 3: Video Editing (Reels/Shorts)

### CapCut (Best Free Option)
1. Download: App Store / Play Store / Desktop (capcut.com)
2. Key features for e-commerce:
   - Auto-captions (text overlay)
   - Green screen
   - Speed ramping (trendy transitions)
   - Trending templates (one-click Reels)

### Reel Creation Workflow:
1. **Film** (phone, natural light, 15-30 sec clips)
2. **Import** to CapCut
3. **Edit**:
   - Trim + arrange clips
   - Add trending audio
   - Add text overlays
   - Speed up/slow down moments
   - Add transitions
4. **Export**: 1080x1920, 30fps
5. **Upload** to Instagram Reels

## Step 6: Content Ideas Generator

### Never Run Out of Ideas — Use These Frameworks:

**Framework 1: E-commerce Content Pillars (Rotate)**
1. Product Showcase (40%) — New, bestsellers, styling
2. Social Proof (25%) — Reviews, UGC, testimonials
3. Behind Scenes (20%) — Packing, sourcing, team
4. Education/Tips (15%) — Size guide, care, styling tips

**Framework 2: Weekly Content Theme**
| Day | Theme | Example |
|-----|-------|---------|
| Mon | Motivation Monday | Inspiring parenting quote |
| Tue | Trending Tuesday | Use trending audio/format |
| Wed | Wisdom Wednesday | Clothing care tip / size guide |
| Thu | Throwback/New Thursday | New arrival or throwback style |
| Fri | Feature Friday | Customer spotlight / review |
| Sat | Sale Saturday | Weekly offer / flash deal |
| Sun | Story Sunday | Engagement stories (polls, Q&A) |

**Framework 3: Steal Ideas From**
- Competitor Instagram accounts (not copy, adapt)
- Pinterest trending pins in kids fashion
- TikTok trending formats (adapt to Reels)
- Customer questions (turn FAQs into content)
- Seasonal moments (festivals, back to school, birthdays)

## Step 7: Photo & Video Tips (Phone Only)

### Smartphone Photography Setup:
- **Lighting**: Near a large window (natural light)
- **Background**: White poster board or bedsheet
- **Props**: Wooden crate, flowers, soft toys
- **Camera**: Use portrait mode for products
- **Editing**: Snapseed or Lightroom Mobile (free)

### Product Photo Checklist:
- [ ] Clean product (steam/iron)
- [ ] White/clean background
- [ ] Good natural lighting (no shadows)
- [ ] Multiple angles (front, back, detail)
- [ ] Size reference (show on kid or flat-lay with ruler)
- [ ] Close-up of fabric/texture
- [ ] Tag visible in one shot

### Video Tips:
- Film in 4K or 1080p
- Hold phone steady (use cheap tripod ₹300)
- Film horizontal for YouTube, vertical for Reels
- Film 3x more than you need (best clips only)
- Natural light > ring light > no light
- Background music/audio makes average video feel professional
