# 01 — Google Business Profile Setup

## What This Does
Creates your business listing on Google Maps & Search. When someone searches "kids clothing near me" or "Indistylex", your business appears with photos, reviews, hours, and a link to your website.

**Time needed**: 30 minutes setup + 5-14 days for verification

---

## Step 1: Create/Login to Google Account

1. Go to https://accounts.google.com
2. Use your business email OR create a new Gmail (e.g., `indistylex.business@gmail.com`)
3. Complete profile setup

> ⚠️ **TIP**: Use a dedicated business Google account, not personal. You'll share access with team later.

---

## Step 2: Go to Google Business Profile

1. Visit: https://business.google.com
2. Click **"Manage now"**
3. Search for your business name: **Indistylex**
4. If not found, click **"Add your business to Google"**

---

## Step 3: Enter Business Information

### Business Name:
```
Indistylex - Kids Fashion & Clothing
```

### Business Category (Primary):
```
Children's Clothing Store
```

### Additional Categories (add all relevant):
- Clothing Store
- Online Clothing Store
- Baby Clothing Store
- Children's Store

---

## Step 4: Add Location

### If you have a physical store:
- Enter full address
- Pin exact location on map
- Set service area (city/radius)

### If online-only (no physical store):
- Select **"I deliver goods and services to my customers"**
- Choose **"No, I don't have a physical store"**  
- Set service areas: All India / or specific cities you ship to

---

## Step 5: Contact Information

| Field | Enter |
|-------|-------|
| Phone | Your business WhatsApp number |
| Website | `https://indistylex.com` |
| Appointment URL | Leave blank |

---

## Step 6: Verify Your Business

Google will verify you own this business. Methods available:

### Method 1: Postcard (Most Common)
- Google mails a postcard to your address
- Takes 5-14 days
- Contains a 5-digit verification code
- Enter code at https://business.google.com when received

### Method 2: Phone Verification (If Available)
- Automated call/SMS to your business phone
- Enter the code immediately

### Method 3: Email Verification (Rare)
- Only if your domain email matches

> 📝 **While waiting for verification**: Complete ALL remaining steps below. Your listing will go live once verified.

| Category | What to Upload | Minimum |

| Logo | Square logo (400x400) | 1 |
| Cover | Best product collection shot | 1 |

- Natural lighting preferred
- Show product variety (different categories)

## Step 9: Add Products

1. Go to **Products** tab in Google Business
2. Click **"Add Product"**
3. For each product:
   - Name: `Boys Cotton T-Shirt - Blue Stripes`
   - Category: `Clothing`
   - Price: `₹499`
   - Description: Short 2-line description
   - Photo: Product image
   - Link: Direct product URL on your website

> Add your **top 10 bestsellers** here. Update monthly.

---

## Step 10: Set Up Messaging

1. Go to **Messages** tab
2. Turn ON messaging
3. Set welcome message:
```
Hi! 👋 Thanks for reaching out to Indistylex. 
How can we help you today? We typically reply within 1 hour.
```

---

## Step 11: Get Your First Reviews

### Strategy to get initial reviews:
1. After every order delivery, send WhatsApp message:
```
Hi [Name]! 🙏 Hope [Child's name] loves their new outfit! 
Would you mind leaving us a quick Google review? It really helps our small business grow.
[Your Google Review Link]
```

2. Get your **Google Review Link**:
   - Go to Google Business → Home → "Get more reviews"
   - Copy the short link
   - Save it — you'll use this everywhere

### Review Response Templates:

**For 5-star reviews:**
```
Thank you so much, [Name]! 🙏 We're thrilled that your little one loves their outfit. 
Can't wait to style them again! — Team Indistylex
```

**For negative reviews:**
```
Hi [Name], we're sorry to hear about your experience. We take this seriously 
and would love to make it right. Please DM us or call [number] so we can resolve this immediately.
```

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| "Business already claimed" | Click "Request access" — Google will contact current owner |
| Postcard never arrived | Wait 14 days, then request another from dashboard |
| Category not found | Use closest match, add specifics in description |
| Profile not showing in search | Takes 1-3 days after verification to appear |
| Suspended account | Check for policy violations, submit reinstatement appeal |
