## Engagement Tactics

### Daily (10-15 minutes):
- Reply to all DMs within 1 hour
- Like & reply to all comments
- Engage with 10 posts from target audience (moms, parenting pages)
- Share 2-3 Stories

### Weekly:
- Collaborate with 1 micro-influencer
- Run 1 Story poll/quiz
- Share 1 customer testimonial
- Comment on 5 competitor's posts (genuine, not spammy)

### Monthly:
- 1 Giveaway (follow + tag 2 friends + share story)
- 1 Live session (Q&A, new collection reveal)
- Audit: What's working? Double down on top 3 post types
