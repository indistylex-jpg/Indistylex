## Target Customer Personas

### Persona 1: Young Mom (Primary - 70% of buyers)
- **Age**: 25–35 years
- **Location**: Metro & Tier-1 cities (Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Pune)
- **Income**: ₹50K–₹1.5L/month household
- **Behavior**: Active on Instagram, shops online, values quality & style
- **Pain Points**: Hard to find stylish yet affordable kids' clothes; sizing issues
- **Trigger**: Festivals, birthdays, season change, school events

### Persona 2: Gift Buyers (Secondary - 20%)
- **Age**: 20–45 years
- **Relation**: Aunts, uncles, grandparents, friends
- **Behavior**: Search "kids gift", "baby clothes gift", buy during festivals
- **Trigger**: Diwali, Raksha Bandhan, birthdays, baby showers

### Persona 3: Dad Buyers (Emerging - 10%)
- **Age**: 28–40 years
- **Behavior**: Quick decision-makers, buy on weekends, prefer COD
- **Trigger**: Wife sends link, last-minute shopping

## Marketing Funnel

```
AWARENESS (Top)          → Instagram Reels, Google Display, Influencers
    ↓
INTEREST (Middle)        → Retargeting Ads, Email nurture, Blog/SEO
    ↓
DESIRE (Bottom)          → Product pages, Reviews, UGC, Offers
    ↓
ACTION (Conversion)      → Cart, Checkout, Payment
    ↓
LOYALTY (Retention)      → Post-purchase emails, WhatsApp, Loyalty program
```

3. **Indian brand, Indian sensibility** — ethnic + western both
4. **Fast shipping** — 3-7 days pan-India
