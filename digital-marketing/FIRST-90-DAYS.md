## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. ❌ Spending on ads before pixel has data (wait 1 week minimum)
2. ❌ Posting without hashtags or captions
3. ❌ Ignoring DMs and comments
4. ❌ Running ads to cold audience with no social proof
5. ❌ Not tracking UTMs (you won't know what works)
6. ❌ Giving up after 2 weeks (results take 30-60 days)
7. ❌ Copying competitors exactly (be authentic)
8. ❌ Spending all budget on one channel (diversify)
9. ❌ Not collecting emails (owned audience > rented audience)
10. ❌ Poor product photos (invest in good photography first)
