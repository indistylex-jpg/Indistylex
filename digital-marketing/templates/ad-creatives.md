# Ad Creatives & Templates

## Canva Templates to Create

### 1. Instagram Post (1080x1080px)
- Product on white/pastel background
- Brand logo (small, bottom-right)
- Price tag in gold accent
- "Free Shipping ₹999+" badge (top-left)

### 2. Instagram Story (1080x1920px)
- Product photo (center)
- "SWIPE UP" or "TAP TO SHOP" CTA
- Price + discount clearly visible
- Brand colors (black + gold)

### 3. Instagram Reel Cover (1080x1920px)
- Eye-catching text overlay
- Product sneak peek
- Consistent brand font/colors

### 4. Facebook Ad (1200x628px)
- Product lifestyle shot
- Headline text overlay: "Premium Kids Clothing from ₹499"
- Trust badges: "Free Shipping | 7-Day Returns"

### 5. Google Display Ad Sizes
- 300x250px (Medium Rectangle)
- 728x90px (Leaderboard)
- 160x600px (Wide Skyscraper)
- 320x50px (Mobile Banner)

## Graphic Design Specs (For Canva/Designer)

### Brand Assets Always Include:
- Logo: Small, bottom-right corner
- Font: Playfair Display (headings) + Inter (body)
- Colors: Black (#1a1a1a), Gold (#C9A94E), White
- Badge: "Free Shipping ₹999+" (gold on black pill shape)

### Photo Guidelines for Ads:
- Well-lit, natural light preferred
- Kid smiling/playing (happiness sells)
- Show fabric texture (close-up detail shot)
- Clean background (white, cream, or outdoor)
- Multiple angles in carousel

### Don't:
- Use stock photos of Western kids
- Overcrowd with text (max 20% text on image for Meta ads)
- Use low-resolution images
- Use aggressive RED/YELLOW color schemes
