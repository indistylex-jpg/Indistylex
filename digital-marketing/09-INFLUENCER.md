# 09 — Influencer Marketing

## Strategy

### Focus: Micro-influencers (1K-50K followers)
- **Why**: Higher engagement, affordable, authentic, niche audiences
- **Budget**: ₹5,000/month (2-4 collaborations)
- **Goal**: UGC content + brand awareness + sales

### Influencer Types to Target:
1. **Mom bloggers** (parenting content) — HIGHEST PRIORITY
2. **Kid fashion accounts** (styling content)
3. **Lifestyle moms** (daily life + recommendations)
4. **Family vloggers** (YouTube + Instagram)

## Collaboration Models

### Model 1: Product Barter (₹0 cost, just send free products)
- **For**: Nano-influencers (1K-5K followers)
- **What you send**: 1-2 outfits worth ₹1,000-1,500
- **What they deliver**: 1 feed post + 2-3 stories with tags
- **Best for**: Starting out, building UGC library

### Model 2: Product + Small Fee (₹500-2,000)
- **For**: Micro-influencers (5K-20K followers)
- **What you send**: 2-3 outfits + ₹500-2,000 fee
- **What they deliver**: 1 Reel + 1 post + stories + permission to use content
- **Best for**: Quality content, wider reach

### Model 3: Affiliate/Commission (₹0 upfront)
- **For**: Any size influencer
- **How**: Give them unique discount code (e.g., PRIYA10)
- **Commission**: 10-15% on every sale through their code
- **Best for**: Performance-based, no risk
